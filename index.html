The Avengers
Avengers Tower , was Stark Towers before Stark made the tower - the main headquarters of the Avengers post the events of The Battle of NY city. The tower is powered by an Arc Reactor that makes it capable of running itself for over a year. Following the Avengers Civil War, Stark sold the tower and had the valuable items and artifacts loaded onto a cargo plane for transport to the New Avengers Facility.
Location: Manhattan, New York City.
Owner: Tony Stark a.k.a Iron Man
